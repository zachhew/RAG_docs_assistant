# Citations

Citations show which document chunks were used to support the generated answer.

## Why Citations Matter

Citations improve transparency.
They help users understand where the answer came from and reduce blind trust in the language model.

## Citation Fields

Each citation includes:

- chunk_id
- source
- title
- url

## Benefits

Citations make the system more interpretable.
They are useful for:

- debugging retrieval
- validating grounded answers
- improving user trust
- comparing retrieval quality across experiments

## Limitations

A citation does not automatically prove that the answer is correct.
It only shows which chunks were used as supporting context.

## Future Improvements

Later versions of the system may include:

- retrieval score
- reranker score
- highlighted evidence spans
